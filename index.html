The Problem

Nasal Airway Obstruction

Restricted nasal breathing is one of the most common complaints heard by ENTs. In fact, 1 in 8 people experience regular difficulty breathing through the nose. This is a result of nasal obstruction, which is often the result of a narrowing of the internal nasal valve. This condition is a daily source of discomfort that reduces quality of life. Sufferers report shortness of breath throughout the day, difficulty sleeping & habitual snoring, and limited stamina during exercise.

The Solution

Schnozzle Nasal Dilator

Schnozzle is worn comfortably and discreetly in the nasal passages to open the airways and instantly improve nasal breathing. The device is the first intranasal dilator that is suitable for use during the day, and it presents a sought-after alternative to reconstructive surgery.
